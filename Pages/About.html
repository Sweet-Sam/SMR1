Whomst Am I?


Heya, the name's Sam. Long story short I just got too much free time and figured I'd shout my opinions out into the void that is the internet, like any good user of it. Like any other good user of the internet I also know that no one really cares, but I figure I may as well archive my thoughts somewhere and I know for a fact I can't keep a diary or journal.


The cat which I will probably post a whole lot of (the funny little guy in the picture in this page) is Caiman, he's still young, 'round 4 months as of writing this, and he may be an annoying, unbearable asshole sometimes but I still love him at the end of the day, even if I really wish I could sleep through the morning without getting either clawed, chewed or having my ears mewoed out.


More about me though. I like writing, playing games, procrastinating, and I spend the majority of my free time with music in my ears. I'm still not published , but someday, maybe in a few weeks, I'll have a book that's at least halfway decent out there so I can plug it and hopefully make some money, but it's not like I really expect to be able to make any sort of living out of writing unless I get really lucky.

Other than that, I don't know much else to say that I'm not going to go over in my rambles, which outside of the Ramble of The Week, will be posted pretty much whenever I feel like posting one. Don't ask me for a schedule on the weekly one either, I probably won't follow it anyways.


One last thing: the cat that I'm using as a logo right now isn't caiman, I haven't managed to get a pic of him yawning yet. I will change it to a picture I own sooner or later, but until then, don't sue me I don't have any money and I just found that image on google. Peace, and thanks for reading.
